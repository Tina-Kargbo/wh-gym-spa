About Us

What We Do

Thousands of women in Accra, Ghana are in poor health because of a lack of access to health facilities. WHGS is a free gym and spa that welcomes all women to have access to its health facilities in a private secluded environment.

Why We are Free

The WHGS is a Free Gym and Spa, sponsored by the Women's Health Foundation

Free Health Professionals!

Do you want a free gym session with a Personal trainer?, massage session with a Masseuse or physiotherapy session with a physiotherapist? WHGS is open to all women.

Register and Book

In order to access our services and facilities, just register on our form, then contact us and book your free session. WHGS tries to do everything to accommodate all customers, however, we operate on a first come-first served basis.
